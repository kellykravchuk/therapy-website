My Approach

I specialize in developmental work with neurodivergent youth and families through parent coaching and DIR/Floortime‑informed interventions, as well as short‑term therapy with emerging adults. I also work with adults and couples seeking greater connection and ease.

Clinically, I’m interested in how early relationships live on inside of us and show up in the here‑and‑now. I use formulation to make sense of patterns like self‑criticism or people‑pleasing, then pair insight with practical skills for regulation, boundaries, and more authentic connection. I bring a strong interest in curiosity, empathy, compassion (toward self and others), and the shared search for meaning and understanding as pathways to growth.
